Essay, sketch, and print project

Stories, route notes, and handmade prints from the Atlantic edge.

Atlantic Field Notes is an ongoing publishing project gathering writing, sketches, and small print editions shaped by coast paths, harbour towns, chapels, weather, and the practical life of the western seaboard.

What it is A slow publishing project combining essays, field sketches, route journals, and occasional printed editions.
Who it is for Readers, walkers, artists, and coastal obsessives who like attentive writing and grounded visual detail.

Spring issue: chapels, tide lines, and ferry crossings
