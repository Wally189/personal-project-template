State the project plainly

Explain the project in one paragraph, show one strong image, and provide a single onward route. This keeps the site feeling intentional rather than unfinished.

What to include

  • Short origin story or thesis statement.
  • Current stage of the project.
  • One direct invitation to follow updates or get in touch.
